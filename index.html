The Lovely Couple

the beginning

Angelique and Michel met in 2016 through a mutual friend named Ohini. He thought they would make a nice couple and did everything in his power to plan their first meeting at a 5 @ 7 in downtown Montreal. Following their first encounter, the two young lovebirds went on their first date at the Montreal Museum of Fine Arts. A few days later, they planned a full day together in Old Montreal. Surrounded by charming decor, beautiful weather, this day was the day when everything changed for our two lovers. While sitting in front of the Saint Lawrence River, admiring the sumptuous landscape, Michel decided ... not to kiss Angelique! Not only did he not kiss her at that moment, the rain set in and forced our lovers to find refuge at McDonald's! However, it is at the foot of the entrance that the magic occurred and he finally kissed her. This first kiss of course will remain engraved forever in their hearts.

the future

For more than 3 years, Angelique and Michel have shared their love life. Both calm, poised and generous, they got to know each other and discovered their different interests. Angelique aka Angie for friends and family is fascinated by mangas, soca music and sci-fi films. While Michel, known as Mike is a fan of sports, jazz music and frequently maintains a level of cleanliness that can sometimes be qualified as excessive! On May 9, 2020 these two lovely souls will make the decision to unite and spend the rest of their lives together. They wish to make this day an unforgettable one, while in the company of the people who they hold dearest to them.
